Nike Store Data App

How might we improve the store registration process to intake new stores faster?

When a new Nike retail store is going to open, it's important that WHQ is tracking the data associated to that store so we can make sure it is functioning in the proper ways. The current process for intaking this data is through a series of offline spreadsheets and forms in Salesforce. This results in users entering repetitive data throughout the process and misalignment between the geographies & WHQ.

I was tasked with updating this intake process.

AUDIT

I conducted a heuristic audit of the current Salesforce-based UI to understand some of the lower-hanging improvements I could begin with.

INTERVIEWS

I spoke with stakeholders from WHQ, Greater China, and APLA who work in the field and manage the tech implementations of new stores.

WIREFRAMES

I went through several iterations of wireframes with input from the data architecture team and feedback from another UX designer.
