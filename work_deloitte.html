ABOUT THE PROJECT

Problem: Deloitte Insight Studio held a Studio Jam at the University of Michigan in September 2017. The given problem statement asked for a solution targeted at managers of a famed local deli, Zingerman's, as a way to help them manage high demand on football Saturdays in Ann Arbor. Essentially, help Zingerman's better manage the increased demand, while working within a team, in order to present a deliverable to Deloitte Insight Studio at the end of the Jam.


Process:

  • Ideated on the given problem statement and brainstormed with group, considering needs such as customer experience, food supply chain issues, revenue maximization, and our user group of managers.
  • Built journey maps specific to each of these identified needs, supporting them with calculations and data found through research. For example, the journey map for a customer included walking all the way from the football stadium to Zingerman’s deli location (over a mile), waiting in a long line that wraps around the outside of the building, waiting within the line inside of the building, placing orders at an iPad station, proceeding to the cash register to cash out, finding seating at Zingerman’s, and waiting for servers to find where you sat down and bring food out to your table. As you can see, even this single journey has many pain points and much room to improve.
  • Created multifaceted solution set comprising environmental/space design proposals such as moving more of the line indoors and creating a more interactive and pleasing waiting experience (more samples, interesting storytelling about the history of Zingerman’s, etc.), service design improvements such as speeding up the ordering process from a single iPad station to circulating staff throughout the line with iPads to take orders, supply chain revisions, and a dashboard integrating real time and historical data including heat maps of Ann Arbor and past expected revenue to be used by management.

Results: An image of my team’s solution is available in the header. I presented this solution with one other teammate to the audience including Deloitte Insight Studio representatives. I highlighted the customer’s journey as well as the business impacts and predictive models we proposed for managers. Ultimately, this project was selected by Deloitte Insight Studio managers and designers as the winner of the Studio Jam.
